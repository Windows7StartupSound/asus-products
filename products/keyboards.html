Keyboard

The ASUS ROG Azoth keyboard is a premium offering in the world of gaming peripherals, designed for both performance and aesthetics.


With customizable RGB lighting, it enhances any gaming setup while providing tactile feedback and responsive keystrokes for an optimal gaming experience.


Built with high-quality materials, it ensures durability and longevity, catering to both casual and competitive gamers.


The Azoth also features programmable keys and macro support, allowing for personalized configurations and enhanced gameplay efficiency.


ASUS's attention to detail and innovation shines through in the ROG Azoth, making it a top choice for discerning gamers.
